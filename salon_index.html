Our Story

Crafted With
Passion & Precision

Maison Lumière was born from a single belief — that every client deserves a transformative experience, not just a haircut. Our studio blends the artistry of Parisian technique with the warmth of a neighbourhood salon.

Each stylist in our team is hand-selected for their technical mastery and genuine love of the craft. We invest in continuous education so you always receive the most innovative techniques available.
